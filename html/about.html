About Our Website

  • Introduction

    The Master Plan of Delhi-2001 had laid emphasis on the development of sports facilities for all age groups within easy reach of all the sections of society. Hence Atlas Sports Complex has developed sports complex , play-fields, etc. all over Delhi.

  • Sports Management Board (SMB)

    Sports Management Board (SMB) is the policy making body for the Sports Wing. Matters regarding development of public sports facilities, including sports complexes and golf courses, type of facilities to be developed, administration, operations and maintenance of the facilities are decided by the SMB.

    The composition of the SMB is as follows:

    1. Hon'ble Lt. Governor Chairman
    2. Vice Chairman, DDA Member
    3. Member (Incharge of Sports Wing), DDA Member
    4. Engineer Member, DDA Member
    5. Commissioner (Sports), DDA Member Secretary

  • Objectives

    The objectives of Atlas Sports Complex are :-

    To provide recreational and healthy environment to the citizens of Delhi.
    To take sports to those sections of the society which cannot afford membership of prestigious clubs and those who would like to associate with 'clubs' totally devoted to sports.
    To promote various sports not easily accessible to the general public.
    To ensure mass participation in sports activities and thereby the spirit of mutual help and goodwill among citizens of Delhi in general and the members of the complexes in particular.

    The land over which public sports complex of Atlas have been built is the property of Atlas. All facilities built thereon, are wholly owned and controlled by Atlas. There are no statutory provisions, rules and regulations, bye-laws, etc., entitling participation in the management and affairs of these sports facilities by any person, institution, agency, etc., other than Atlas and no person with membership/playing rights of these facilities shall have any kind of rights on the property, management and affairs of the atlas sports complex , The control and management of these will also be with the Atlas.

  • Membership

    The sports complex membership oriented. Various types of membership are available to the public. Public may also avail facilities on “pay and play” basis. The maintenance of the complex is essentially from member's monthly subscription / pay and play fees and allied charges received from licensing out of snack bars, pro shops, etc. Rules and Regulations for the Sports Complex have been formulated and membership is approved by a Screening Committee nominated for the complex.
